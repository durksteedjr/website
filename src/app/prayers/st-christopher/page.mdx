# St. Christopher

## English

Great St. Christopher,<br />
You sought the mightiest master whom You found in Jesus Christ,<br />
The Almighty God of Heaven and earth.<br />
Faithfully serving Him with all Your power<br />
To the end of Your life,<br />
You gained for Him countless souls,<br />
Ultimately shedding Your blood for Him.<br />
Obtain for me the grace to bear Christ always in my heart,<br />
As You once did, bearing Him on Your shoulder,<br />
Carrying the weight of the world.<br />
