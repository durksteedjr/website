---

# {frontmatter?.title}

## English

Out of the depths, I call to You Lord;<br />
Lord, hear my cry! May Your ears be attentive to my cry for mercy.<br />
If You, Lord, keep account of sins, Lord, who can stand?<br />
But with You is forgiveness and so You are revered.<br />
I wait for the Lord, my soul waits and I hope for His word.<br />
My soul looks for the Lord more than sentinels for daybreak.<br />
More than sentinels for daybreak, let Israel hope in the Lord,<br />
For with the Lord is mercy, with Him is plenteous redemption.<br />
And He will redeem Israel from all its sins.

## Latin

De profundis clamavi ad te, Domine;<br />
Domine, exaudi vocem meam. Fiant aures tuæ intendentes in vocem deprecationis meæ.<br />
Si iniquitates observaveris, Domine, Domine, quis sustinebit?<br />
Quia apud te propitiatio est; et propter legem tuam sustinui te, Domine. Sustinuit anima mea in verbo ejus:<br />
Speravit anima mea in Domino.<br />
A custodia matutina usque ad noctem, speret Israël in Domino.<br />
Quia apud Dominum misericordia, et copiosa apud eum redemptio.<br />
Et ipse redimet Israël ex omnibus iniquitatibus ejus
