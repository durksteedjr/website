# {frontmatter?.title}

St. Ignatius of Loyola

## English

Receive, Lord, all my liberty,<br />
Take my memory, my understanding, and my entire will.<br />
All I have and call my own, You have given all to me;<br />
I give it all back to You<br />
and surrender it wholly to be governed by Your will.<br />
Give me only Your love and Your grace,<br />
and I am rich enough and ask for nothing more.

## Latin

Suscipe, Domine, universam meam libertatem.<br />
Accipe memoriam, intellectum atque voluntatem omnem.<br />
Quidquid habeo vel possideo, mihi largitus es;<br />
id tibi totum restituo,<br />
ac tuae prorsus voluntati trado gubernandum.<br />
Amorem tui solum cum gratia tua mihi dones,<br />
et dives sum satis, nec aliud quidquam ultra posco.
